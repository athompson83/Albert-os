# Experiment 2: Government Contract Opportunity Briefs

## Market Research

### Target Buyers
Small businesses that sell services to government:
- IT firms (cybersecurity, cloud, software)
- Cleaning/janitorial companies
- Staffing agencies
- Training providers
- Security firms
- Medical supply companies
- Construction trades
- Consulting firms

### Public Data Sources (Free)
1. **SAM.gov** — System for Award Management (federal contracts)
2. **Florida VBS** — Vendor Bid System (state contracts)
3. **Lee County Purchasing** — Local government contracts
4. **City of Fort Myers** — Municipal contracts
5. **Collier County** — County-level contracts

### Sample Brief Format

**CONTRACT OPPORTUNITY BRIEF**
- **Agency:** [Name]
- **Solicitation #:** [Number]
- **Title:** [Contract Title]
- **NAICS Code:** [Code]
- **Set-Aside:** [Small Business / 8(a) / HUBZone / etc.]
- **Estimated Value:** [$ Range]
- **Response Deadline:** [Date]
- **Description:** [2-3 sentence summary]
- **Requirements:** [Key requirements]
- **Go/No-Go Recommendation:** [Yes/No with reasoning]
- **Competition Level:** [Low/Medium/High]
- **Next Steps:** [Register, contact agency, prepare bid]

### Pricing Model
- **Basic:** $99/month — Weekly email with 5-10 curated opportunities
- **Standard:** $197/month — Daily briefs + capture packet for top 3 opportunities
- **Premium:** $497/month — Full bid support + proposal writing assistance
- **One-time:** $500-$2,500 per capture packet (full bid readiness package)
