# Experiment 3: Accounting Firm AI Advisory Workflow

## Market Research

### Target Buyers
Small accounting/bookkeeping firms (5-50 employees) that:
- Serve small business clients
- Are under pressure from AI tools (QuickBooks AI, etc.)
- Want to move from compliance to advisory
- Have 50-500 business clients

### The Problem
- SMBs are using AI for financial/tax advice before talking to accountants
- 92% of SMBs would pay higher fees for improved service
- Accountants are losing clients to AI tools
- Firms need to differentiate through advisory services

### The Offer
"AI-Enhanced Advisory Workflow for Accounting Firms"

**What we deliver:**
1. **AI Client Intake Assistant** — Automated onboarding questionnaire that gathers financial data, goals, and pain points before the first meeting
2. **Document Collection Automation** — System that requests and organizes client documents (bank statements, receipts, tax returns) automatically
3. **Monthly Advisory Report Template** — AI-generated insights based on client financial data (cash flow trends, tax optimization opportunities, benchmarking)
4. **Client FAQ Knowledge Base** — Automated answers to common client questions (deadlines, deductions, filing requirements)
5. **Tax Season Workflow** — Automated document request sequences, status updates, and filing reminders
6. **AI-Safe Client Response Policy** — Guidelines for clients on when to use AI vs. when to call their accountant

### Revenue Model
- **Setup:** $500-$1,500 one-time (depending on firm size)
- **Monthly:** $197-$497/month (based on number of clients)
- **Per-client:** $5-$10/client/month for the advisory workflow

### First Deliverable: Sample Workflow
Create one complete sample workflow for a 200-client accounting firm:
- Client intake form (Google Forms or Typeform)
- Document collection automation (email sequences)
- Monthly advisory report template (Google Docs)
- Client FAQ (Notion or similar)

### Outreach Strategy
1. Find 30 small accounting firms in SW Florida
2. Send personalized email with free workflow audit
3. Offer free 1-week trial of client intake automation
4. Convert to setup fee + monthly retainer
