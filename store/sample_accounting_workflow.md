# SAMPLE: Accounting Firm AI Advisory Workflow
**Prepared by Hermes AI — For Small Accounting/Bookkeeping Firms**

---

## The Problem Your Clients Are Facing

Your small business clients are using AI tools (ChatGPT, QuickBooks AI, etc.) to get financial advice, tax answers, and business insights — often before they talk to you.

**The risk:** They act on generic AI advice that doesn't account for their specific situation, then come to you to fix the problems.

**The opportunity:** Position your firm as the trusted advisor who validates AI output and provides personalized guidance.

---

## The Solution: AI-Enhanced Advisory Workflow

### 1. AI Client Intake Assistant

**What it does:** Before a new client's first meeting, they complete an intelligent questionnaire that gathers:
- Business type, size, and structure
- Current financial pain points
- Tax concerns and deadlines
- Goals for the next 12 months
- Current software/tools in use

**How it works:**
- Client receives a link (Google Form or Typeform)
- Questions adapt based on answers (if they say "LLC", ask LLC-specific questions)
- Responses are organized into a client profile document
- You receive a summary before the meeting

**Time saved:** 30-45 minutes per new client intake

---

### 2. Document Collection Automation

**What it does:** Automatically requests and organizes client documents throughout the year.

**How it works:**
- Email sequences request documents at key times:
  - January: W-2s, 1099s, prior year tax return
  - March: Q1 financials, estimated tax payments
  - June: Mid-year financial review documents
  - September: Q3 financials, tax planning documents
  - December: Year-end tax planning checklist
- Documents are organized in a shared folder (Google Drive)
- Reminders sent automatically for missing documents

**Time saved:** 2-3 hours per client per tax season

---

### 3. Monthly Advisory Report Template

**What it does:** AI-generated insights based on client financial data.

**Report sections:**
- **Cash Flow Summary:** Month-over-month trends, upcoming cash needs
- **Tax Optimization:** Estimated tax liability, deduction opportunities, quarterly payment reminders
- **Benchmarking:** How the client compares to industry averages
- **Action Items:** 3-5 specific recommendations for the next 30 days
- **Questions for Your Accountant:** Items to discuss at next meeting

**How it works:**
- Export client data from QuickBooks/Xero
- AI generates insights and recommendations
- Accountant reviews and personalizes
- Client receives branded PDF

**Value add:** Transforms your firm from "tax preparer" to "trusted advisor"

---

### 4. Client FAQ Knowledge Base

**What it does:** Automated answers to common client questions.

**Common questions answered:**
- "When are estimated tax payments due?"
- "Can I deduct my home office?"
- "What receipts do I need to keep?"
- "Should I incorporate my business?"
- "What's the difference between an LLC and S-Corp?"

**How it works:**
- Knowledge base hosted on your website or Notion
- Clients search or browse questions
- Reduces phone calls and emails by 30-40%
- Frees up staff time for higher-value work

---

### 5. Tax Season Workflow

**What it does:** Automated tax season communication and tracking.

**Workflow:**
1. **Early January:** "Tax season is here" email with document checklist
2. **Mid-January:** Reminder for missing documents
3. **February:** "Your return is in progress" update
4. **March:** "Your return is ready for review" notification
5. **April:** Filing confirmation and next-year planning reminder

**Time saved:** 5-10 hours per week during tax season

---

### 6. AI-Safe Client Response Policy

**What it does:** Guidelines for clients on when to use AI vs. when to call you.

**Policy template:**
- **Use AI for:** General tax questions, basic bookkeeping guidance, industry research
- **Call us for:** Specific tax advice, complex transactions, audit support, business decisions
- **Always verify:** AI-generated tax advice should be verified by a professional before acting

**Why it matters:** Positions you as the expert while acknowledging AI tools. Reduces liability from clients acting on bad AI advice.
